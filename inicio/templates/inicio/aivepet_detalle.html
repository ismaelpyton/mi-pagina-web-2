AIVEPET

As a Technical and Linguistic Specialist for AIVEPET.SRL (November 2024 – February 2025) on the critical SKY2102-VII machine project, my work focused on providing comprehensive and multifaceted support. I delivered expert, high-precision trilingual technical interpretation services (Mandarin, English, and Spanish), facilitating effective communication and collaboration between international and local technical teams. Furthermore, I coordinated complex technical operations, ensuring project cohesion and progress within a high-pressure environment. This overarching intervention was decisive in overcoming significant obstacles and ensuring the project's ultimate success, which culminated in positioning AIVEPET.SRL as the pioneering company in the Dominican Republic to offer Octane Rating (MOR and RON) measurement services—a milestone for the company and the national energy sector.

The core technical challenge of the project was a critical operational failure of the SKY2102-VII machine, which would neither power on nor respond, generating considerable urgency and scrutiny from the Ministry of Energy and Mines. To address and resolve this issue, I applied my foundational knowledge in electrical circuits, fluid mechanics, and materials engineering, acquired during my education at INTEC, enabling my active participation in diagnosing the root cause. The resolution involved the precise translation of complex technical documentation and the facilitation of clear communication for executing necessary repairs, culminating in the successful commissioning and restoration of the machine's full functionality.
